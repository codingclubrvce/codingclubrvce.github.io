Our Aim

R V Coding Club was started in the year 2016 by six students from the ECE department with an aim of bridging the gap between the departments when it comes to competitive coding. Then known as InSYNC, the R V Coding Club bloomed from a small team of 10 individuals to a capacity of over 60 students today, spanning across the branches of CSE, ISE, ECE, EEE and Mechanical among others.

Today the Coding Club operates over various domains with work being done in Machine Learning, Web Development, Image Processing and Database Management Systems among others in addition to competitive programming. The club and its students are interested in taking up projects from the industry to work on and build their profiles. Weekly sessions are conducted to help the students receive a better understanding of topics of importance.

The Coding Club aims to have its students take part and win in Hackathons and coding events such as the GSOC and the Google Code Jam in addition to developing Software Tools which would serve for the betterment of the society as a whole. The club members have worked on some of the most technically advanced projects done in the college with respect to the Computer and Information Sciences.

Our Vision

To give students the skills, confidence and opportunity to change their world using coding and help them become successful in GSoC , ACM-ICPC and other recognized coding competitions.

Our Mission

To develop coding skills among the students and incorporate a coding culture.
To mentor students with Google recognized organizations and help them contribute to open source
To give opportunity to each and every student of the club to crack globally recognized competitions such as Google Summer of Code , Google Code Jam and ACM ICPC
