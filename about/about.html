The Coding Club is backed heavily by the faculty of the college including the Heads of the Departments of CSE, ISE and ECE. The ISE HoD, Dr. B M Sagar is very actively involved in the working of the club and in the conduction of events associated with the club. The faculty advisor Prof. Priya D went about the entire process of setting up the club and takes active involvement in the functioning of the club. Prof. Girish Rao Salanke is an advisor and mentor of the coding club providing his valuable inputs and feedback in the conduction of events by the club.
